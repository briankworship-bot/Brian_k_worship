About

Brian K Worship is a Pastor, worship leader, and an all-round musician with a special calling in gospel ministry. He is a saxophonist with deep knowledge of multiple instruments including guitar, bass, keyboard, and solo guitar.He was taught by Rev Father Wijnand Huys and Pro Mugulusi Paul in Mbiko Brass Band where he started his music carier at the catholic church of st Kalori Lwanga

Having ministered across South Africa and other parts of the world, Brian K now resides in Uganda and is available for global ministry and music engagements.
